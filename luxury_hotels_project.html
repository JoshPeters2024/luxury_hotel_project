Data Visualizations

Bar Chart Analysis

Average Hotel Score by Theme: In this bar chart, you will find which themes tend to have the highest scores in relation to the top luxury hotels. The manor and lodge theme placed in the bottom of this list, potentially indicating that their nature reliant and old timey nature tend to hurt their score compared to the top themes, such as the tropical island theme, or the botique hotels, which are often very fashionable. However, with scores being close, it is evident that theme is not a major factor in seperating average hotel scores. Value may be found in a person trying to determine a new type of hotel they want to visit.

Average Hotel Score by Region: In this chart, we find that the hotels from Southeast Asia has the highest average hotel rating for luxury hotels, while the Carribean has the lowest. While the difference is slight, it is still important to note the highest scoring regions for luxury hotels as people may be looking for hotels based on certain areas of the world that they want to visit, and this may help narrow that list down.

Average Hotel Score by Country: The number one takeaway from this visualization is the observation that the Maldives have the highest average luxury hotel rank by a fiarly significant margin, with an average score of 99.02. This is 1.0733 points higher than the next closets countries average score. If a traveler is looking specifically for the nicest possible hotel, this may lead them to booking a trip to the Maldives.
